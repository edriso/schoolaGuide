Exist Problems

  • If parents want to enroll in a scholl for their son, they have to go to the school itself, or they search for a parent that his son studying there to ask them!
  • So they spend a lot of time and money in transpotations to just get information about 1 school!
  • They don't know all the schools around them, so you find some people enroll in a far school
  • If they found a feedback it usually be from few parents to many of them, so may be the majority love the school but they knew one of the few that didn't liked the school!

Our Solution

  • We made a responsive website about schools that could be accessed on any device whether it's a pc, laptop or smartphones
  • Now parents can know everything they want about any school
  • In anywhere they are in just a min
  • They also can select only the schools in their neighborhood
  • They can also select which type or stage of schools they want
  • And for sure they can search for a specific school!
  • They also can see others parents feedback about school
  • And they can help other parents by say their feedback about the school that their kids in!
  • So a lot of time and efforts are saved, and also feedback plus all the information they need are all now in Schoola
